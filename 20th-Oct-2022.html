Bible Text: John 14:14

Imagine that as the Class Representative, you were called upon to distribute test scripts to your classmates and everyone is calling for your attention at the same time. I’m sure you would get confused and not know who to answer first. This is just very little compared to what Jesus goes through every day. People call on Him from different parts of the world every second. However, as busy as He is, when any of His children calls on Him, He stops, pays attention and attends to their needs. My child, Jesus listens whenever you call on Him. These days, many issues persist because Christians learn and read about prayer more than they pray. Another reason problems persist in the lives of children of God is that, rather than talk to God about them, they grumble or complain to friends and family. Psalms 34:15 says: “The eyes of the LORD are on the righteous, and his ears are open unto their cry.” Is there any issue you have been battling with for so long? This is the time to talk to Jesus about it because He listens. ACTION POINT Spend time in talking to God about the things bothering you today. HYMN WHAT A FRIEND WE HAVE IN JESUS 1. What a friend we have in Jesus, All our sins and griefs to bear; What a privilege to carry Ev’rything to God in prayer Oh! what peace we often forfeit, Oh! what needless pain we bear All because we do not carry Everything to God in prayer. 2. Have we trials and temptations? Is there trouble anywhere? We should never be discouraged, Take it to the Lord in prayer Can we find a Friend so faithful Who will all our sorrow share? Jesus knows our every weakness, Take it to the Lord in prayer. 3. Are we weak and heavy laden, Cumbered with a load of care? Precious Saviour still our refuge, Take it to the Lord in prayer Do thy friends despise, forsake thee? Take it to the Lord in prayer In His arms He’ll take and shield thee, Thou wilt find a solace there. BIBLE READING IN ONE YEAR: 1 TIMOTHY 4:1-16, JEREMIAH 11-13
